Birla Divya Jyoti School

With a sprawling campus situated at a picturesque estate, the School has separate blocks for Pre-Primary, Primary, Middle and Senior Sections with Prayer Halls & Auditoriums. Its spacious Classrooms have been designed to optimise Natural Light and Ventilation which is Specially designed with Modern, Ergonomic Furniture to ensure the comfort of every Child. BIRLA DIVYA JYOTI SCHOOL aims to provide a number of facilities for your Toddlers through a unique learning method incorporating Outdoor & Indoor Activities.

• One of the finest schools in Siliguri, poised to make a Difference.

• Education through the finest blend of Indian and Global Learning.

• School at the Doorstep for the Kids of Uttorayan & Surrounding.

BIRLA DIVYA JYOTI SCHOOL follows CBSE Curriculum, imparting World-class Education along with Modern Facilities. The School offers a Pragmatic and Qualitative all round Education to its Students. It has created a considerable scope for its Students to Learn & Practise a wide variety of Co-curricular Activities. It has been designed with Spacious & Airy Classrooms supported by Computer Aided Learning Facilities in the Classrooms, Well-equipped State-of-the art Laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Home Science & Computer Science, Well-stocked Libraries, Audio-visual Room(s), Playground for Outdoor Games, Gymnasium for Indoor Games and Swimming Pool.

SARALA BIRLA GROUP OF SCHOOLS was the dream project of Late Sarala Birla, wife of Late B. K. Birla, an eminent educationist who made immense contributions in the field of Education by opening Schools across India. This group has a successful track record of running some of the Finest Educational Institutions across the Country. BIRLA DIVYA JYOTI SCHOOL is a co-educational English Medium School at UTTORAYON TOWNSHIP, SILIGURI, set up by BIRLA VIDYA VIHAR TRUST under the aegis of the BIRLA GROUP.
