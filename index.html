Birla Divya Jyoti

BIRLA DIVYA JYOTI is a co-educational English medium school at Uttorayon Township,Siliguri set up by Birla Vidya Vihar Trust under the aegis of the BIRLA GROUP. The group has a successful track record of running some of the finest educational institutions across the country and is committed to quality education. The school which follows the CBSE curriculum imparts world class education with modern facilities.


The school offers a pragmatic and qualitative all round education to its students. It has been designed with spacious and airy classrooms supported by computer aided learning facilities in the classrooms, well-equipped state-of-the art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Home Science and Computer Science, well-stocked libraries, audio-visual room(s), playground for outdoor games, gymnasium for indoor games and swimming pool.

Enlightening Souls with Quality Education

We all know that education and learning is a continuous and lifelong process. We at Birla Divya Jyoti are committed to provide a safe and intellectually vibrant environment that will empower the students to become innovative thinkers, problem solvers, creative and inspired life long learners in order to thrive in the 21st Century and be a global citizen in true sense.

Our Founders

Born on 23th November, 1923, wife of Late B. K. Birla was a prominent member of the Birla Family of Indian industrialists. She not only endeavoured a remarkable exertion as an Indian Business Woman through putting visionary ideas into Business Development, but also as an eminent Educationist through opening Schools across India, which are among the Best. Smt. Sarala Birla was a lady with a Prismatic Personality. Her Ethical Values, her Unconditional Giving, and her Charm encouraged thousands to follow her righteous conceptions. Her legacy of Values and her Spirit will Live on with us forever.
